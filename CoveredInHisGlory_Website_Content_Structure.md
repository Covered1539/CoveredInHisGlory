## DEVOTION PAGE — `/devotion`

### Page Header
**Eyebrow:** Look. Remember. Live.
**Headline:** Today's Devotion
**Subhead:** A small word for an ordinary day.

### Devotion Entry Template (use this format for every entry)
```
[DATE]

"[Scripture quote, full verse]"
— [Book Chapter:Verse]

[3-4 short paragraphs, 80-150 words total. Conversational,
warm, not preachy. Should connect the verse to something the
reader is likely feeling that day — weariness, fear, waiting,
grief, distraction — and end with hope, not just information.]

[Closing line — always: "Look. Remember. Live."]
```

### Sample Entry (already written — ready to publish)
> **Monday, June 22**
>
> *"He will cover you with his feathers, and under his wings you will find refuge; his faithfulness will be your shield and rampart."*
> — Psalm 91:4
>
> There is a difference between being told you're covered and actually feeling it. Some mornings the weight of what you're carrying makes the second one hard to believe.
>
> This verse doesn't say He covers you from a distance. It says *feathers* — close, soft, near enough to feel. Refuge isn't a place you have to travel to find. It's wherever you are right now, the moment you stop trying to shield yourself and let Him do it instead.
>
> Whatever today is asking of you, you don't have to carry it uncovered.
>
> Look. Remember. Live.

### Devotion Archive
Display as a simple reverse-chronological list below the current entry: date + first line of Scripture + "Read more" link. No need for categories or tags — simplicity keeps this feeling like a journal, not a blog.

### Production Plan
| Cadence | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| Frequency | 2-3x per week minimum; daily if sustainable |
| Batch writing | Write 7-14 devotions at once, schedule to auto-publish |
| Source material | Pull from your existing product Scripture foundations (Psalm 91:4, 23:4, 52:8, Proverbs 7:3) and rotate outward from there |
| Length | Keep every entry under 150 words — this is a pause, not a sermon |

## PRAYER WALL PAGE — `/prayer`

### Page Header
**Eyebrow:** You Are Not Carrying This Alone
**Headline:** Share What's On Your Heart
**Subhead:** Tell us privately and let only us pray with you — or share it on the wall and let this whole community stand with you. Either way, it will be read, and it will be prayed over.

### Two Tabs / Toggle: "Private Request" and "Prayer Wall"

#### Tab 1 — Private Request Form
**Fields:**
- Your First Name *(required)*
- Your Email *(optional — label: "only if you'd like a note back")*
- What's on your heart? *(required, textarea)*
- Checkbox: "Keep my name fully anonymous, even to your team"

**Submit button:** Send This Privately
**Confirmation message:** "Your prayer request has been received — you are prayed over."
**Note under form:** Read by our team only. Never shared, never sold, never public.

**Backend handling:** Submissions route to a private inbox (e.g., grace@coveredinhisglory.com or a dedicated prayer@coveredinhisglory.com) — NOT published anywhere. Consider a simple weekly rhythm of personally praying through the inbox.

#### Tab 2 — Public Prayer Wall
**Submission form fields:**
- Display Name *(required — label: "First name, or 'Anonymous'")*
- Your Prayer Request *(required, textarea)*

**Submit button:** Post to the Prayer Wall
**Confirmation message:** "Thank you for sharing — your request will appear after a gentle review."
**Note under form:** Every post is reviewed gently before it goes live — never delayed more than a day.

**Wall display:** Reverse chronological list of approved requests, each showing:
- Display name
- Time posted ("2 hours ago" / "Yesterday" / "3 days ago")
- The request text
- A "🙏 I'm praying for this" button with a running count

**Moderation requirement:** All public posts must pass through manual approval before appearing. This protects against spam, inappropriate content, and gives you the chance to personally read and pray over every single one before it's public — which is itself a ministry act.

### Technical Note for Implementation
A simple moderated form (Squarespace forms → email notification → you manually copy approved ones into a wall, OR a lightweight tool like Tally/Fillout feeding into Airtable with an "approved" checkbox that displays publicly) is sufficient at launch. No need for user accounts or login.

## SEO & TECHNICAL NOTES

| Item | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| Page titles | "Covered in His Glory — [Page Name] — Faith-Based Handmade Gifts" |
| Meta description (homepage) | "Handcrafted prayer blankets, faith backpacks, and Scripture-rooted gifts. Faith woven into every detail — Numbers 15:39." |
| Image alt text | Always describe + include Scripture reference, e.g. "Cream crochet prayer blanket with Psalm 91:4 Scripture tag" |
| Mobile | Every section must be tested and confirmed readable on a 375px-wide screen before launch |
| Forms | All forms need a spam honeypot field or CAPTCHA — prayer wall is a likely spam target given its open submission |
| Load speed | Compress all product photos before upload — target under 300KB per image |
| Accessibility | All interactive elements (tabs, buttons) need visible focus states; form labels must be programmatically associated with inputs |
