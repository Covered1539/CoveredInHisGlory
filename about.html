Our Story

Faith woven into every detail.

"That you may look upon it and remember all the commandments of the LORD and do them."

— Numbers 15:39

The Meaning Behind the Name

Covered — because you are sheltered, protected, and held. Not from a distance, but close enough to feel. In His — because none of this is ours to claim; it belongs to Him. Glory — because every ordinary thing we make is meant to point back to something far greater than itself.

Covered in His Glory began with a simple conviction: that faith shouldn't only live in the words we say on Sunday, but in the things we hold, wear, and carry every day of the week.

Our Foundation — Numbers 15:39

"Speak to the Israelites and say to them: Throughout the generations to come you are to make tassels on the corners of your garments… You will have these tassels to look at and so you will remember all the commands of the LORD, that you may obey them." Numbers 15:37–40

God gave His people something to see — a physical reminder woven right into their clothing — so that remembering would become a way of living. That is the whole heart of what we do. Every blanket, backpack, cushion, and keepsake is a modern tassel: something you can look at, so you remember, so you live it out.

Our Mission

We believe faith should be woven into every detail of life — not only spoken, but lived, worn, and carried. We create handcrafted, Scripture-rooted pieces that place God's Word where you can see it in the middle of an ordinary day, and let it steady you in an extraordinary one.

What We Create

  • Prayer Blankets — crochet blankets with corner Remembrance Tags, for comfort and covering.
  • Faith Backpacks — for carrying His truth through every valley.
  • Kneeling Cushions — a set place to pause, kneel, and set your heart on Him.
  • Sacred Reminders — wearable Scripture and keepsakes that keep His Word close.

Our Commitment

  • Everything is made by hand, one at a time — never mass-produced.
  • Every piece begins with a Scripture, not a SKU.
  • Each item is prayed over before it ships.
  • We create knowing what we make may become a cherished heirloom, a source of daily encouragement, or a lifeline in someone's darkest hour.

Every stitch points hearts back to the Lord.
