Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Covered in His Glory ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using this website, you agree to the terms below.

Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: your name, email address, and message content when you submit a contact form, custom order request, prayer request, or newsletter signup.
  • Prayer requests: details you choose to share. Private requests are read only by our team. Public prayer wall posts are reviewed before they appear and may be displayed publicly using the display name you provide.
  • Automatically collected data: basic, non-identifying technical information (such as browser type and pages visited) that our hosting provider may log to keep the site secure and running.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your messages, orders, and requests.
  • To pray over the requests you send us.
  • To send our newsletter, only if you subscribe. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • To operate, protect, and improve the website.

How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers that help us run the site and communicate with you (for example, our form-handling and email providers), and only as needed to provide those services. We may disclose information if required by law.

Third-Party Services

Forms on this site are processed by a third-party form service (Formspree) and delivered to our email. Fonts are served by Google Fonts. If you visit our shop on Etsy or our social media pages, those platforms have their own privacy policies that govern your use of them.

Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to respond to you, fulfill your request, or meet legal obligations, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

Your Choices

  • You may request a copy of, correction to, or deletion of your personal information.
  • You may unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time.
  • To make any of these requests, email us at grace@coveredinhisglory.com.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted here, with the "last updated" date revised accordingly.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Email grace@coveredinhisglory.com.
